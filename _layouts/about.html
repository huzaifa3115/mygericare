About Us

Gericare Ltd. is medical practice based in Park Ridge, IL and specializes in Internal Medicine, Geriatric Medicine and Wound Care. Our practice believes in a team approach and is fully supported by board certified geriatricians, physician assistants, nurse practitioners, nurses, and diligent ancillary staff members. The practice provides care to patients in the outpatient setting and for convenience of the patient/family, we travel locally to provide care at skilled nursing facilities, assisted living facilities, memory care and independent living facilities.

Gericare Ltd practice providers can tap into a depth of experience within our own practice; we are committed to providing respectful, accommodating, and prompt care to our patients. All of our providers, in addition to being board certified, are credentialed and affiliated with Advocate Aurora Healthcare. This association requires our providers to adhere to the highest quality healthcare outcomes and hold the practice accountable to the highest standards in the medical community.

Our team believes in strong partnerships in the community. That is why we are affiliated with Advocate Aurora Healthcare. Caregivers play a special role in the health and quality of life of our patients. Please ask us for help if you need respite care, part time care, or advice!

Please call our office if you have questions about the services at a particular facility. Our best advice is: if we can't care for you or your loved ones, ask the nurses which doctor they would choose!
