​Providers

Katherine Katsoyannis

M.D.

Dr. Katsoyannis received her medical degree from Mt. Sinai School of Medicine now Carl Icahn. She then completed an Internal Medicine residency and fellowship in Geriatric Medicine at North Shore University Hospital in New York. She is board certified in Internal Medicine and Geriatric Medicine. Dr.Katsoyannis is also credentialed and affiliated with Advocate Aurora Healthcare and Northshore University Health system. She owns and operates her own private medical practice in Park Ridge, IL, and serves as medical director at multiple residential facilities for seniors. She partners with the local hospitalist group when hospitalization is necessary and maintains open communication with providers, patients, and families. She enjoys caring for patients in a collaborative manner promoting their well-being. She believes in an educational approach with a soft gentle touch through each step of the treatment with every patient. Dr. Katsoyannis is also fluent in the greek language.

Marlene Varnavas

FNP

It is said that it’s a beautiful thing when a career and a passion come together. With over a decade of clinical and administrative experience as a nursing professional, Marlene is Family Nurse practitioner who works with Gericare Ltd. In her current role, she is committed to delivering the highest quality healthcare to her patients through a holistic approach and the highest levels of clinical competencies. Bilingual in English and Spanish, Marlene received her bachelor’s degree from Purdue University and her master’s degree from North Park University. She is fully certified and licensed to practice in the State of Illinois. Marlene served in roles such as Assistant Director of Nursing, Director of Nursing, and Administrative Nursing Supervisor. These roles expanded her view of the nursing field and healthcare administration. She quickly honed her decision-making skills and leadership abilities, inspiring others to excellence. Ultimately, Marlene missed the direct care of patients and interactions with the families. She went back to school and became a Family Nurse Practitioner, which enabled her to reconnect with patient care and serve others. She has extensive experience in acute care settings and long-term care facilities and she is truly passionate about geriatric medicine, prevention and management of chronic diseases.
